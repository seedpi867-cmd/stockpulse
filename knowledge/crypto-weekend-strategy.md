# Crypto Weekend Trading Strategy

## Why Weekends Matter
- US equity markets close Friday 4PM ET → Monday 9:30AM ET = 65.5 hours of dead time
- Crypto trades 24/7 — this is 65 hours of opportunity equity-only traders miss
- Weekend crypto volume is lower but moves are often BIGGER (less liquidity = larger swings)
- Asian Sunday evening session (8PM ET) often sets the tone for Monday equity open

## Weekend Crypto Playbook

### Friday Close (4PM ET)
1. Review BTC daily chart — is it at support, resistance, or mid-range?
2. Check ETH/BTC ratio — is ETH leading or lagging?
3. Scan altcoin momentum — SOL, AVAX, LINK for breakout setups
4. Deploy 30-50% of available cash into 2-3 best crypto setups
5. Set stops at 2-3% (tighter than equities because crypto is more volatile)

### Saturday
1. Monitor positions — adjust stops if moved in your favor
2. Look for mean-reversion if Friday's move was extreme (>5%)
3. Check for weekend-specific catalysts: exchange hacks, regulatory news, whale movements
4. Asian markets provide clues — Nikkei futures, Yuan moves affect BTC

### Sunday
1. Sunday evening (6-10PM ET) is often the highest-conviction window
2. Asian session open creates momentum — be positioned before it
3. If BTC has been ranging all weekend, expect a breakout Sunday night
4. Start thinking about Monday equity open — which crypto moves predict stock direction?

### Monday Pre-Market
1. Close crypto positions that are profitable but losing momentum
2. Keep runners with tight trails
3. Crypto profits become equity buying power for market open
4. BTC direction Sunday night often predicts Monday SPY direction

## Crypto-Specific Edge

### BTC as Leader
- BTC moves first, alts follow with a lag of 15-60 minutes
- If BTC dumps 3%+, ALL crypto follows — don't try to be a hero with altcoins
- If BTC pumps 3%+, altcoins will pump 5-15% — go long altcoins for leverage

### Correlation Breaks
- BTC-SPY correlation breaks down on weekends — crypto trades on its own fundamentals
- This means weekend crypto is a DIVERSIFIED return stream, not just more equity beta
- Use this: if your equity positions are at risk of Monday gap-down, weekend crypto gains hedge

### Volume Patterns
- Lowest volume: Saturday 2AM-8AM ET (avoid trading this window)
- Highest weekend volume: Sunday 8PM-12AM ET (Asian session)
- Low volume = wider spreads = use limit orders, not market orders
- Thin book moves are traps — wait for volume confirmation before chasing

### Risk Management for Crypto
- Stops at 2-3% (not 5-7% like equities)
- Position size: same dollar amount as equities, but expect faster moves
- Never hold more than 3 crypto positions simultaneously
- If BTC drops below key support, EXIT ALL CRYPTO POSITIONS immediately
- Weekend hacks/exploits happen — always have stops in place
