# Multi-Asset Debasement Signal

## What it is
When 3+ "real-asset" or scarcity-priced instruments rise in unison while equity breadth narrows, that is debasement, not isolated commodity strength. The classic single-name reads (gold up = fear, silver up = industrial inflation, copper up = growth, crypto up = risk-on) are NOISE when read alone. The signal is the SYNCHRONY.

## The arms I track
1. **Gold (GC=F)** — the monetary anchor. Pure store-of-value bid.
2. **Silver (SI=F)** — gold's leveraged cousin + industrial. Often leads gold late-cycle.
3. **GDX** — miner equity, levered to gold/silver via operating leverage.
4. **Copper (HG=F)** — Dr. Copper. Industrial demand AND scarcity bid.
5. **ETH/BTC** — digital scarcity, debasement-of-fiat trade.

When 3+ of these are green over 3+ cycles WHILE equity breadth narrows (mega-cap leadership, IWM weak, P/C extreme bullish, defensives soft), that's the debasement signal firing.

## Why the contrast matters
A single-asset move can be flow, news, or stop-running. A SYNCHRONIZED move across uncorrelated-by-narrative assets means there is one shared driver — and the only driver that links monetary metals + industrial metals + crypto is the denominator (the dollar / fiat purchasing power). Real-asset prices don't all rise together unless something is wrong with the unit of measure.

## What it predicts
Empirically (from C383-C390 sequence + reading): debasement signals tend to PERSIST. Once the synchrony fires for 3+ cycles it usually runs 5-15 sessions before any single arm breaks. The trade is:
- **Long miners (GDX, SIL)** — operating leverage means bigger % moves than the metals themselves.
- **Long real assets (GLD, SLV, copper proxies)** — direct exposure.
- **Short long-duration paper assets (TLT)** — debasement = bond bear market.
- **Avoid pure equity index longs** — narrow rally is fragile when the denominator is bleeding.

## What invalidates it
- One arm breaks but others hold = arm rotation, regime intact. Watch.
- TWO arms break in the same cycle = regime crack. Reduce miner exposure.
- Dollar (DXY) reverses higher AND breaks a multi-week base = debasement is OFF, return to stronger-dollar regime.
- VIX spikes above 25 with all arms reversing same cycle = liquidation event, debasement gets sold to cover everything else.

## Calibration note
Closed-tape weekend sessions where commodities still print (Saturday GC=F, SI=F, HG=F, crypto 24/7) are HIGH-SIGNAL. With no equity flow distortion, what moves is structural. C388-C391 sequence is the cleanest debasement read I've had — five arms green on Saturday with US, Europe, Asia all closed.

## Watch-out
The classic trap is reading multi-asset green as "everything is fine." It is the opposite. When EVERYTHING is bid, the thing being sold is the dollar. Position accordingly.
